What This Is

The Penny Method is an awesome way of practicing all sorts of things. First, you start out with 5 pennies, and everytime you complete something, such as a speedrun trick or basketball free-throw, you move one of the pennies to the side (instead this adds a checkmark). But, if you miss a trick or shot, then you have to reset and move all the pennies back over and start doing it again.
